Food Warming Mat Cleaning & Maintenance

Keep your warming mat performing optimally with proper cleaning and maintenance. Learn the best practices for long-lasting performance.

Quick Tip: Your food warming mat is dishwasher safe and easy to clean. Always unplug and allow 15 minutes cooling time before cleaning for safety.

Cleaning Your Warming Mat

1

Safety First: Power Off and Cool Down

Before cleaning your food warming mat, always follow these critical safety steps:

  • Unplug the power cord: Disconnect the 110V power supply completely
  • Separate components: Disconnect the touch panel from the heating panel
  • Allow cooling time: Wait at least 15 minutes for the warming mat surface to cool down completely

Warning: Never clean the warming mat while it is plugged in or still hot. This can cause electric shock or burns.

2

Dishwasher Safe Cleaning

The silicone heating pad of your food warming mat is dishwasher safe for easy, convenient cleaning:

  • Place on top rack: Position the silicone heating pad on the upper rack of your dishwasher
  • Use normal cycle: Run a standard dishwasher cycle with regular detergent
  • Air dry completely: Allow the silicone pad to air dry fully before reconnecting

Note: Never put the control panel or power cord in the dishwasher. Only the silicone heating pad is dishwasher safe.

3

Hand Washing Method

For quick cleaning or when you prefer hand washing your warming mat:

  • Prepare cleaning solution: Mix warm water with a small amount of mild detergent or dish soap
  • Use soft cloth or sponge: Gently wipe the heating pad surface with the damp cloth
  • Rinse thoroughly: Wipe with clean water to remove all soap residue
  • Dry completely: Wipe dry with a clean towel or allow to air dry

Pro Tip: For stubborn stains or stuck-on food, apply a small amount of toothpaste to the affected area and gently rub with a soft cloth. The mild abrasive in toothpaste effectively removes stains without damaging the silicone surface.

4

Cleaning the Control Panel

The control panel requires special care to prevent electrical damage:

  • Wipe only: Use a slightly damp cloth to wipe the exterior of the control panel
  • Keep it dry: Never submerge the control panel in water or put it in the dishwasher
  • Avoid liquids entering: Do not allow water to drip into button openings or connection ports
5

What NOT to Use

Avoid these cleaning products and methods to prevent damage to your food warming mat:

Prohibited Cleaners:

  • • Bleach or chlorine-based cleaners
  • • Paint thinner or toluene
  • • Acidic cleaners or vinegar
  • • Lamp oil or kerosene
  • • Alcohol or chemical solvents
  • • Abrasive scrubbers or steel wool

Avoid These Methods:

  • • Submerging control panel in water
  • • Using high-pressure spray
  • • Scrubbing with rough materials
  • • Cleaning while still plugged in
  • • Using sharp objects to scrape
  • • Applying excessive pressure

Proper Storage & Maintenance

Storage Guidelines

When storing your warming mat for extended periods, follow these best practices:

  • Clean before storing: Always clean and dry the warming mat completely before putting it away to prevent mold or odors
  • Store flat or rolled: The silicone heating pad can be stored flat or gently rolled without creasing
  • Keep in dry location: Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture
  • Protect from heavy objects: Don't place heavy items on top that could damage the heating elements
  • Coil cord properly: Wrap the power cord loosely without tight bends or kinks

Regular Maintenance Tips

Extend the life of your food warming mat with these simple maintenance practices:

  • Inspect before each use: Check for cracks, damage, or wear on the heating pad and control panel
  • Check power cord: Examine the cord for fraying, cuts, or exposed wiring before plugging in
  • Clean after each use: Wipe down the surface after every use to prevent buildup
  • Keep connections clean: Ensure the connection between control panel and heating pad is free from debris
  • Unplug when not in use: For long periods of non-use, unplug to prolong service life and save energy

Quick Cleaning Reference Guide

For Light Cleaning:

  • 1. Unplug and wait 15 minutes to cool
  • 2. Wipe with damp cloth and mild detergent
  • 3. Rinse with clean water
  • 4. Dry completely before use

For Deep Cleaning:

  • 1. Unplug and disconnect control panel
  • 2. Place silicone pad in dishwasher (top rack)
  • 3. Wipe control panel with damp cloth only
  • 4. Air dry all components completely
