What Fits on the Mat?

The mat fits a large dish plus a few smaller items. Use these examples as a guide and adjust for your cookware.

Cookware Typical Fit Notes
9" × 13" Casserole Pan Fits comfortably Leave a small gap for airflow near the controller.
2 to 3 Medium Serving Bowls Fits with overlap-free spacing Use lids to retain heat longer.
Plates & Small Dishes Fits multiple plates Stagger plates to maximize space.

Layout Tips

  • Keep dishes flat for full contact with the mat.
  • Place heavier items closer to the center of the mat.
  • Leave space around the controller for ventilation.
  • Use compatible heat-safe cookware only.

Table Protection

  • Use the built-in feet to create a cooling air gap.
  • Avoid stacking items on top of each other.
  • Keep liquids away from the controller connection.
  • Roll and store only after the mat cools down.
