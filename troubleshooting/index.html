Food Warming Mat Troubleshooting

Quick solutions to common problems. Get your warming mat working properly again with these troubleshooting tips.

Need Help? Most warming mat issues can be resolved with simple troubleshooting. Check the solutions below for your specific problem. If issues persist, contact customer support.

Common Problems & Solutions

Problem: No Heating / Warming Mat Not Heating

The warming mat is powered on but does not heat up, or food stays cold.

Possible Causes & Solutions:

  • Cause 1:

    Control panel not properly connected to heating pad

    Solution: Unplug the device, check that the control panel connector is firmly inserted into the heating pad port. Reconnect securely and plug back in.

  • Cause 2:

    Power cord not fully plugged into outlet

    Solution: Check that the power plug is completely inserted into a working 110V outlet. Test the outlet with another device if necessary.

  • Cause 3:

    Temperature set too low or device in standby mode

    Solution: Press the power button to activate heating mode. Adjust temperature higher using the temperature control knob.

  • Cause 4:

    Child lock activated preventing operation

    Solution: Press and hold the lock button for 1.5 seconds to deactivate child safety lock.

  • Cause 5:

    6-hour auto shut-off activated

    Solution: If the warming mat has been running continuously for 6 hours, it automatically enters standby. Press power button to restart heating.

Problem: Display Light Not Working

The temperature or timer display is not illuminated or showing readings.

Possible Causes & Solutions:

  • Cause 1:

    Device not powered on or in standby mode

    Solution: Press the power button to activate the warming mat. The display should light up with temperature reading.

  • Cause 2:

    Poor connection between control panel and heating pad

    Solution: Unplug device, disconnect and reconnect the control panel to the heating pad firmly, then plug back in.

  • Cause 3:

    No power supply to device

    Solution: Verify outlet is working, check power cord connections, and ensure plug is fully inserted.

  • Cause 4:

    Control panel malfunction or damage

    Solution: Contact customer support at 13410039885@163.com for repair or replacement options.

Problem: All Display Lights Flashing

All indicator lights on the control panel are blinking or flashing continuously.

Possible Causes & Solutions:

  • Cause 1:

    Temperature sensor malfunction or connection issue

    Solution: Turn off and unplug the device. Wait 30 seconds, then reconnect control panel firmly to heating pad. Plug in and restart.

  • Cause 2:

    Overheating protection triggered

    Solution: Unplug immediately and allow the warming mat to cool for at least 30 minutes. Check for obstructions on heating surface. Ensure proper ventilation.

  • Cause 3:

    Internal circuit error or component failure

    Solution: If flashing persists after reset, discontinue use and contact customer support for professional diagnosis and repair.

  • Cause 4:

    Damage to heating pad or control panel

    Solution: Inspect for visible cracks or damage. Do not use if damaged. Contact support at 13410039885@163.com for replacement parts.

Problem: Key/Button Failure

Buttons are unresponsive, not registering presses, or working intermittently.

Possible Causes & Solutions:

  • Cause 1:

    Child safety lock is activated

    Solution: Press and hold the lock button for 1.5 seconds to deactivate child lock. All buttons except power should then work normally.

  • Cause 2:

    Dirt, grease, or debris blocking button contacts

    Solution: Unplug device. Clean control panel surface with slightly damp cloth. Ensure buttons are free from residue and dry completely before use.

  • Cause 3:

    Button press not firm or long enough

    Solution: Press buttons firmly and hold for at least 0.5 seconds. Some functions (like lock) require 1.5 seconds press duration.

  • Cause 4:

    Control panel electronic malfunction

    Solution: Try a complete power reset: unplug for 5 minutes, then reconnect. If problem persists, contact customer support for repair.

  • Cause 5:

    Physical damage to button mechanism

    Solution: Inspect for cracks or broken buttons. Do not attempt self-repair. Contact manufacturer for authorized replacement control panel.

Problem: Uneven Heating or Cold Spots

Some areas of the warming mat are hotter or cooler than others.

Possible Causes & Solutions:

  • Cause 1:

    Dishes not evenly distributed or too large

    Solution: Position dishes evenly across the heating area. Ensure dishes are within the designated heating zone for optimal heat distribution.

  • Cause 2:

    Debris or water on heating surface blocking heat transfer

    Solution: Clean the heating surface thoroughly. Remove rice grains, crumbs, or liquid before placing dishes.

  • Cause 3:

    Warming mat on uneven or heat-absorbing surface

    Solution: Place warming mat on a flat, stable, heat-resistant surface. Avoid metal countertops or surfaces that absorb heat.

  • Cause 4:

    Heating element damage or degradation

    Solution: If heating remains uneven after cleaning and proper placement, the heating element may be damaged. Contact customer support for inspection.

Problem: Odor or Smell From New Warming Mat

New warming mat emits a slight plastic or chemical smell during initial uses.

This is Normal:

A slight odor when first using your food warming mat is completely normal and expected. This is due to:

  • • New silicone and electronic components heating for the first time
  • • Manufacturing residues burning off during initial heating cycles

Solution: Run the warming mat for 15-20 minutes at medium temperature without food in a well-ventilated area. The odor should dissipate after the first few uses. If smell persists beyond 5 uses or becomes stronger, contact customer support.

When to Contact Customer Support

If you experience any of the following, discontinue use immediately and contact customer support:

  • Physical damage: Cracks in heating pad, broken control panel, or exposed wiring
  • Electrical issues: Sparking, burning smell (not normal new-product odor), or electric shock
  • Persistent malfunctions: Problems that don't resolve after troubleshooting steps
  • Overheating issues: Excessive heat, melting, or surface deformation
